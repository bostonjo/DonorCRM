# Donor Management App - User Manual

## Overview
The **Donor Management App** is a centralized tool for the Southwest Corridor Park Conservancy to track donations, manage donor relationships (households), and monitor fundraising projects. It provides a real-time analytics dashboard, comprehensive data entry tools, and export capabilities.

---

## 2. Navigation
The sidebar provides access to the five main sections of the application:
- **Dashboard**: High-level view of fundraising performance.
- **Donations**: Detailed list and management of individual donations.
- **Households**: Directory of donors and their contact information.
- **Projects**: Management of fundraising campaigns/projects.
- **Export**: Tools to download data for external use.

> **Tip:** Use the **Back** button at the top left (when visible) to return to your previous view after drilling down into details.

---

## 3. Analytics Dashboard
The landing page provides an immediate snapshot of fundraising health.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Raised**: Gross revenue for the selected period.
- **Donations**: Total number of individual transactions.
- **Avg. Donation**: Average dollar amount per gift.
- **Unique Donors**: Number of distinct households that contributed.
*(Clicking on these cards helps navigate to the detailed Donations view)*

### Charts
- **Donations Over Time**: Visual timeline of giving.
- **By Project**: Breakdown of funds across different projects (click a slice to drill down).
- **Payment Methods**: Distribution of Checks, Venmo, PayPal, etc. (click a slice to drill down).

### Dashboard Filters
You can refine the data shown on the dashboard using the controls at the top:
- **Year Mode**: Switch between specific years, "All Time", or a "Custom Range".
- **Project**: Filter by a specific project.
- **Method**: Filter by payment type (e.g., Check, Venmo).
- **Min. Amount**: Use the slider to exclude small donations below a certain threshold.

## 4. Donation Management
This section is for logging, viewing, and editing donation records.

### Viewing Donations
- **Sorting**: Click column headers (Date, Household, Amount, etc.) to sort the table.
- **Filters**:
    - **Year**: Same controls as the Dashboard.
    - **Household**: Search for a specific donor to see only their gifts.
    - **Method**: Filter by payment type.

### Logging a New Donation
1. Click **Log New Donation** (top right).
2. **Household Search**: Start typing the donor's name. Select them from the dropdown list.
    - *Note: You must select an existing household. If they are new, add them in the "Households" section first.*
3. **Amount & Date/Method**: Enter the donation details.
4. **Project**: Assign the gift to a specific campaign (optional).
5. **Comments**: Add any relevant notes.
6. Click **Save Donation**.

### Editing or Deleting
- **Edit**: Click the **Pencil Icon** on the right side of a donation row to modify details.
- **Delete**: Click the **Trash Icon** to remove a record (requires confirmation).

---

## 5. Household Directory
Manage your database of donors here.

### Directory Features
- **Search**: Find households by Name or Address.
- **Refine Filters**:
    - **Donation Year**: Find donors who gave in a specific year (or "Never Donated").
    - **Has Email**: Toggle to show only households with email addresses on file.
- **Sorting**: Order cards by Name, Lifetime Giving ("Total Given"), or Recency ("Recent Gift").

### Household Cards
Each card displays:
- **Household Name**: Auto-generated (e.g., "John & Jane Doe") or custom.
- **Lifetime Stats**: Total amount given and number of gifts.
- **Members**: Names and contact info for all individuals in the household.
- **Address**: Primary mailing address.

### Actions
Click the **three-dot menu** on any card to:
- **Edit**: Update members, address, or custom name.
- **History**: View a filtered list of all donations from this household.
- **Delete**: Remove the household (permanently).

### Adding a New Household
1. Click **Add New Household**.
2. **Add Members**: Enter First Name, Last Name, Email, and Phone for each person. Click "Add Member" for couples/families.
3. **Address**: Enter the shared mailing address.
4. **Household Name**: This is auto-generated based on member names but can be manually overridden if needed (e.g., "The Smith Family Trust").
5. Click **Save Household**.

---

## 6. Projects
Create and track specific fundraising initiatives (e.g., "Annual Fund 2024", "Park Bench Restoration").
- **Add Project**: Click "Add Project" and enter a Name and Description.
- **Edit/Delete**: Use the three-dot menu on project cards.

---

## 7. Data Export
Download your data for mail merges, accounting, or backups.

### Steps to Export
1. **Filter Data**: Choose which records to include (e.g., "2024 Donations" or "All Donors from Boston").
2. **Select Record Type**:
    - **Donations**: Transactional data (Date, Amount, Donor Name).
    - **Households**: Donor contact info (great for mailing lists).
    - **Projects**: List of active projects.
3. **Preview**: The table at the bottom shows a sample of what you will get.
4. **Download**:
    - **Excel**: Best for spreadsheets and reporting.
    - **CSV**: Best for importing into other software.
